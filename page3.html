Play a simple but deeply addictive game where you try to make as many paperclips as possible. It starts innocently but gets surprisingly deep and complex — don't say we didn't warn you!

Draw lines and watch a sledder ride your custom track. A creative and fun physics sandbox that lets your imagination run wild. Build huge jumps, loops, and crazy tracks!
